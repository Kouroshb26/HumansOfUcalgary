About

University of Calgary is a vibrant community, with individuals hailing from many different backgrounds. All have diverse perspectives on life, love, learning, and everything in between.

Our objective is to share the stories and photos of students, faculty, alumni, and staff from UCalgary and create a greater sense of community.
